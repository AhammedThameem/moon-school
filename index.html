Empowering Every Child to Shine

At Moon School of Creative Science, we believe that education is not just about academics — it's about building character, developing creativity, and strengthening faith. Our campus is a safe, vibrant space where children love to learn.

We combine modern pedagogical methods with strong value systems to create well-rounded individuals who are ready for tomorrow's world. From our youngest Pre-KG learners to our Grade 7 students, every child receives personalized attention.

As part of our enriched syllabus, Quran Hifz (memorization) is woven into the daily routine — alongside Tajweed and understanding of meaning — allowing students to internalize the words of Allah while excelling in academics. Age-appropriate Hifz goals are set for each level, with dedicated hours, supportive memorization techniques, and regular revision circles to ensure retention and spiritual connection.
